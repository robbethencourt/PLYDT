Get started using plydt!

To get started, click on a location of interest. From there you will be presented with the name of the location and how many, if any, plydters are present at the location. You will also be presented with an option to either pin yourself or view a commentsection with any comments left by pldters at the location. If you decide to pin yourself you will have to input a username, choose if you are a mom or dad, and provide some basic age and gender info about your child along with a timeframe in which you are able to have a playdate.
